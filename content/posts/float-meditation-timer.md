I built a meditation timer app called
[Float](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/float-meditation-timer/id6757253072). It's
on the App Store now, and it's free.

Why build yet another meditation app? Honestly, I just wanted
something simple. Every meditation app I tried came loaded with guided
sessions, subscription upsells, social features, achievement
badges. This stuff seems antithetical to the whole point of sitting
quietly for a few minutes.

Float does three things:

1. Sets a timer
2. Plays a gentle sound when you're done
3. Logs your minutes to Apple Health

That's it. No accounts, no subscriptions, no daily streaks
guilt-tripping you. No notifications pestering you to meditate. No
gamification, no ads, no in-app purchases. Just a timer.
