## Norns

[Norns](https://nornscode.com) — An open-source durable execution runtime for AI agents on the BEAM.

Built in Elixir on the Erlang VM. If an agent crashes mid-run, Norns replays its event log and picks up where it left off. [Read more](/posts/introducing-norns/).

## Chronicle

[Chronicle](https://chronicle.work) — A shared history for your recurring meetings.

Track conversations in 1:1s, team syncs, and other meetings where you don't want to forget what happened. [Read more](/posts/introducing-chronicle/).

## Float

[Float](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/float-meditation-timer/id6757253072) — A simple meditation timer for iOS. [Read more](/posts/float-meditation-timer/).

## Shotify

[Shotify](https://shotify.codes) — Beautiful code screenshots from your editor.

Supports Emacs, VS Code, Neovim, and the CLI. Available as an `npm` package for integrating into your own tools. [Read more](/posts/introducing-shotify/).
